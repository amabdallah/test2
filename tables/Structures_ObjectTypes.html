Table: ObjectTypes

An Object Type is the building element of a data structure that modelers customize its attributes to represent a distinct water system component (e.g., reservoir or a river) in their models (data structures). An Object Type may represent physical and existing water components or to represent conceptual objects like representing water gains and losses in a river though a virtual node object at a point on a river.
