Table: Mapping

It is the central table in WaM-DaM which maps out data values to their attributes, metadata, and organizing structure (i.e., MasterNetworks, Scenarios, ObjectTypes, Instances, and DataStructure). Therefore the MetadataMapping table is often used to query data from WaM-DaM database.
